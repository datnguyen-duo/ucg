Private investing is about who you know. As real estate industry veterans, we have fostered deep relationships with leading sponsors across the country, and we are the first call when they have a new, exclusive offering.

Our Sponsor Requirements:

  • Differentiated, defensible strategy

Confidential

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member?

Fill out the membership request form and then we will reach out to set up an intro call. We value getting to know each of our members and like to learn about your investment preferences, so we can curate offerings for you.

How much does it cost to become a member?

It is free to become a member. Sponsors compensate Uncommon for introductions, at no cost to you.

What is a typical investment minimum for an offering?

The minimum investment varies per sponsor and offering. Typically minimums range from $250,000 to $1,000,000 or more.

Do I invest with Uncommon or the sponsor?

Uncommon provides insider access to sponsors and then your investment is directly with the sponsor. They handle your funds and will provide updates on the performance of your investments.
